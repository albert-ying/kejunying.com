AI-Driven Discovery

ClockBase Agent

An autonomous AI system that mines large-scale molecular profiles to discover aging interventions. ClockBase Agent reanalyzed 43,602 intervention-control comparisons across multiple aging biomarkers, identifying 500+ compounds that significantly reduce biological age. Featured on MIT Technology Review.
